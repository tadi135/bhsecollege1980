About Sri Vidya College

A premier institution committed to academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth since 1995.

BHSRC Degree & Junior College is a NAAC A+ accredited institution established with the vision of providing quality education accessible to all. Over three decades, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region, known for our academic rigor, values-based education, and outstanding placement record.

Our campus is home to dedicated faculty members, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a vibrant community of over 3,500 students. We offer both Intermediate (Junior College) and Degree programs, ensuring students have a seamless academic journey under one roof.

🎓 Affiliated to Osmania University & TSBIE
